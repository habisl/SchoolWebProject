INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON ENGINEERING EDUCATION-2013

TURKU,FINLAND

REGISTRATION

The International Conference on Engineering Education 2012 iNEER(ICEE 2012) was arranged in Turku, Finland, on July 30 - August 3, 2012. The main theme of the conference was Contributing to Success through Innovations in Engineering Education. ICEE 2012 was the 2012 edition of the popular ICEE conference series sponsored around the world by the iNEER Network with 38,000 members in 98 countries. More than 240 colleagues from 32 different countries participated in the ICEE 2012 event. These pages provide information on the past event.


ACCOMODATION AND TOURS

In addition to the topical program and the conference materials, the full registration fee included lunches, refreshments and social events. The social events formed an important part of the ICEE 2012 experience and provided opportunities to discuss and network with colleagues and, naturally, to enjoy the Finnish hospitality and local culture.

On Tuesday evening the City of Turku hosted a reception and wished all the conference participants welcome to Turku and Finland. The event was arranged in the lobby and located in the very heart of the city.Visit the conference website info@icee2012.fi.
